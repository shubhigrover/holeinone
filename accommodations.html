Accommodations


Our hotel offers a range of luxurious accommodations designed to suit every traveler’s needs, ensuring a relaxing and memorable stay. The Deluxe Twin Bed Rooms are perfect for friends or colleagues traveling together, these spacious rooms feature two comfortable twin beds, modern amenities, and stylish décor for a restful retreat.

The Deluxe Double Bed Rooms are ideal for couples or solo travelers seeking extra comfort, these rooms are equipped with plush double beds, elegant furnishings, and all the essentials for a cozy stay.

Our studio suites provide a touch of luxury with open-plan layouts, premium bedding, and additional space to unwind. Perfect for guests who desire both comfort and convenience.

For a more secluded and tranquil experience, our charming cottages offer a home-away-from-home feel. Surrounded by natural beauty, these cottages provide privacy, spacious living areas, and exceptional comfort. No matter your preference, each of our accommodations is thoughtfully designed to ensure your stay is both relaxing and unforgettable.
